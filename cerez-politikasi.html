This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Nest Moment uses them on this website, and the choices available to you. It should be read together with our Privacy & Data Protection Notice.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary: required for the website to function and cannot be switched off.
  • Preferences: remember choices such as your language selection.
  • Analytics: help us understand how visitors use the site. Activated only with your consent.
  • Marketing: used to measure and improve our advertising. Activated only with your consent.

3. Cookies We Currently Use

Today our website uses only strictly necessary and preference storage.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third parties whose services we use.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

A banner lets you accept or reject non-essential cookies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time.
